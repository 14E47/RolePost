People Management is one thing that intrigues a lot of us, but Micky Chopra is the right person if you really want to understand the nuances of this skill. He has built a career in Human Resource management but this was not his natural instinct when he was in his college.

Micky started his professional career with Indian Navy Services, after completing his MCA from Galgotia College (Lucknow University). The technical knowledge he received in the college came in handy for understanding client requirements and this helped him move to FCS Software. While working as the resource manager at FCS, he was able to understand how it is important to be able to manage people to improve processes.

The journey towards Human Resource was a slow and steady transformation, where he with his goal oriented focus was able to carve his way to becoming a talent specialist. His first break in Talent management was with SAVY Infotech as the Country Manager in Talent Acquisition and Client Delivery.  Talent Acquisition was an exciting domain for him but his desire to dwell deeper into the realms of people management made him see beyond talent acquisition.

For last three years, he working with OSS cube and then Taction Software LLC has taken a strategic role, where he understand that talent acquisition is just the start of the cycle and you need to look deeper to sustain the bottom line. He acknowledges how retention and talent engagement can lead to a sustained growth of an organisation.  He approaches human resources in a very technical way and attributes his success to his technical understanding, which has helped him build his career.

The way HR has changed with time is phenomenal,   from being a backend role it has today become a strategy based position with multiple elements like talent branding, talent satisfaction, talent performance and business acquisition through talent being the key matrices today. As Taction Software deals in Niche Technologies, the task becomes more challenging and Talent Branding plays a very important role in acquiring the right talent.

The biggest motivators for Micky have been his family who supported this tough decision of moving from the more lucrative technology domain to people management.  Today, he is amongst the top 50 HR Technical leaders in the country and a very active speaker about technology in HR. He also promotes sports in his work sphere and at Oss cube was the president of the sports initiative and helped build a culture for sports. This he adds also helps build better work ethics.

Born to farmer family, living in a small village in Madhya Pradesh, Vinay could have never imagined in his childhood that we will in future become an inspiration for many in his village.

Vinay Khare was bought up in a small village Akola in Datiya District. The village had very limited resources and most families were engaged in farming. His primary schooling happened in a school in Akola, but his father used to teach him at home. This helped him prepare for the district level exams and got selected in the District Government School. He had secured 3rd rank overall in the Tehsil and was able to fulfil his aspirations for higher education.

The 5km one way walk every day to the school and being a part of new school both made his vision even more focussed on higher education. The biggest challenge he recalls is that children at that time were more afraid of schools and the hard work required he, on the other hand, got motivated by the challenges.

After completing the secondary education till the eighth standard, he had to move to the city Datiya for higher education and gave another entrance for “Saraswati Vidya Mandir, Datiya”. He secured his admission in the school and completed his schooling from this school. He was amongst the bright students and was always amongst the top ranking students.

The biggest glitch in his education so far was that he had always been in a Hindi Medium School and now for higher education English was a must. Through the AIEEE entrance examination, he got admission in Bansal College of Engineering, Mandideep (Bhopal), but the first year was very difficult as most of books and literature was in English. He had to struggle for an entire year to understand the language but his focus made him fight all odds and within the next year, he was back as the top scorer. In the Third year, the biggest opportunity came to his college as Infosys had come for placement. Infosys had a very stringent process of recruitment and he went through multiple interview process. He was academically very strong but with limited command on English, he gave his interview in part Hindi and part English.

But language could not stop his dreams from coming true as he got selected to Infosys, being the only student in his college to get a placement with Infosys. This lead to a new life, and he during his Infosys Training excelled in all tests and also enhanced his English in the process.  After getting his job confirmed in Pune, he was highly content with his life and became an inspiration for a lot of youngsters in his village.

The people who first reach the summit always leave a track for others to follow. Vinay wanted to help people and help every child to fulfil his dream for education. With this in his mind, he joined a Non –Profit called SNEH Foundation. He got associated in Dec 2011, the major purpose of his involvement was to make parents understand how education can help make kids support families financially and also help create a better live standards for the child. He has in these 4 years of work with SNEH Foundation has changed and impacted 1000+ people lives and will in future help many become like Vinay Khare and help inspire others.

This is a chain which he has started and this chain can help our country achieve our goal of educating every single kid in this country.
